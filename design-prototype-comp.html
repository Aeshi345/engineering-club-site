SME Design & Prototype Competition

The SME (Society of Manufacturing Engineers) student chapter organizes an annual SME Design & Prototype Competition, aimed at providing an accessible, low-cost, and educational design experience for undergraduate students.

Unlike large national competitions that require substantial funding and large teams, this event enables students to participate individually in a focused design-and-build challenge. Each year, students will be provided with a machine description and a set of technical specifications, requiring them to independently design, fabricate, and assemble a functional prototype using additive manufacturing (3D printing).

On the competition day, participants will operate their machines for performance-based evaluation by referees. This competition offers significant educational value by strengthening students' skills in engineering design, CAD, manufacturing, prototyping, and problem-solving, while also fostering creativity and innovation under real-world constraints. This event directly supports hands-on engineering education, workforce development, and early engagement with highly motivated students.
