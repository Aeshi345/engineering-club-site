How To Join

Steps to acquire Society of Manufacturing Engineers (SME) membership through CCSU student chapter:

  1. Go to sme.org and click "Become a Member"
  2. Select "Student Membership"
  3. Add membership demographics...
  4. Select your grade in college...
  5. The price should be only $20.

To be a member of SME student club at CCSU, follow the steps below now. It is free.

  1. Go to ClubCentral and sign in with your CCSU credentials
  2. Select "Orgs" and search for "SME"
  3. Press "Join"
  4. An executive member will approve your membership shortly
