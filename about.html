About Us

Central holds Student Chapter for the Society of Manufacturing Engineers (SME).

Our mission is to build practical engineering skills, inspire innovation, and create a welcoming space for collaboration. We host competitions, workshops, technical talks, and community outreach events aimed at preparing students for real-world engineering challenges.

We believe in teamwork, creativity, and professional growth. Every event is designed to connect members with the latest tools, technologies, and opportunities in manufacturing and engineering.
